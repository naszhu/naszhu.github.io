About

I’m Lea, a cognitive scientist interested in building quantitative models that link internal cognitive processes to observable behaviour. I develop probabilistic and dynamical frameworks (e.g., REM, DDM) and evaluate them with carefully designed behavioural experiments. I earned my Ph.D. at Indiana University and currently work as an academic researcher.
